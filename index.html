About me

Hi! I am a Junior Front End Developer, based in Stockholm, Sweden, originally from Skopje, Macedonia. I create clean, semantic and standards-compliant HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I write code that is modular, efficient, and easy to understand by other developers, plus I write excellent documentation. Also, most importantly I enjoy and find it important to make sites responsive for all screen sizes and devices.

I have a long professional and educational background in finance, and I worked as a Business controller for 4 years. But ever since I switched to coding I feel like I have found my true calling because the tech development environment is so exciting and I enjoy collaborating with back end developers and product designers to create something that end user enjoy to use. I get back so much more than juggling financial numbers.

I am a self-taught coder, through courses online such as Coursera, Codecademy, Free Code Camp and many more. My mentor is also my husband who is a software engineer. My first internship as a Front End Developer was at the very exiting start up FundedByMe where I developed my coding skills even more and enjoyed working in a team with other developers, business developers and salespeople.
